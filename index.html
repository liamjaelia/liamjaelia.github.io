See-Food Project Proposal

Select images to see within mobile frame dimensions.

SEE-FOOD is an interactive application focused on providing a tool to enable concious consumption. The experience offered allows users to budget and track their carbon footprint within a singel app.

Upon signing up and launching the application, users craft a personal profile and SEE-FOOD helps to provide targets for consumption. Users will be able to track their purchases and receive scores based upon carbon output. As the correlation between eco-friendly products and higher prices is strong, the application also tracks your budget and financial goals.

Simple graphs and visuals provide important data to users, supporting their desire to consume with more self-awareness
